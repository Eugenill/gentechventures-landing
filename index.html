01 — Mission

Capital is the easy part. The work is the work.

Gen Tech Ventures isn't a fund and it isn't a passive stake. It's a hands-on holding — a long-term home for technology companies where we invest capital and then invest ourselves.

We bring an engineering and executive-leadership background to every company in the portfolio. That means sitting with the team, writing the spec, pressure-testing the architecture, owning the P&L, and standing alongside founders through every hard decision.

Fewer companies. Deeper involvement. Better outcomes.

02 — Vision

Technology, where it matters most.

The next generation of defining companies won't come from another SaaS layer on top of a SaaS layer. They will come from the intersection of software and the physical world — agriculture, sports, industry, and the systems the real economy actually runs on.

Gen Tech Ventures is a small, focused portfolio of companies designed to compound over decades — each one reshaping a real industry with applied technology, disciplined execution, and a serious respect for craft.

We don't optimize for volume. We optimize for depth.

03 — Portfolio

The companies we're building.

Enkitek

enkitek.eu

Smart agriculture for a precision-driven future. Enkitek is a Barcelona-based agtech company building Co-Farmer — an AI-enhanced, IoT-driven platform that turns indoor farms into data-rich, highly automated operations. Through digital twins, real-time environmental monitoring, and intelligent control, Enkitek helps growers in controlled-environment agriculture run more efficient and more productive farms.

Colectin

colectin.com

The marketplace for sports memorabilia. Colectin is a platform where collectors discover, buy and sell sports collectibles — cards, jerseys, autographs, and memorabilia — connecting fans, collectors, and athletes around the items and moments they love.

04 — Founder

Engineer, operator, investor.

Eugeni Llagostera Saltor

Founder · Gen Tech Ventures

Eugeni is an engineer and CEO based in Barcelona. He founded Gen Tech Ventures as a personal vehicle to invest in — and actively help build — technology companies with a long-term mindset. Companies that compound in value by compounding in craft.

He works hands-on inside every company in the portfolio, combining engineering depth with operational experience as a chief executive. From product and technical architecture to strategy, hiring, and the boardroom — he partners with founders on the decisions that matter.

He believes the most interesting technology comes from the intersection of software and the physical world — and that the best companies are built over decades, not quarters.
